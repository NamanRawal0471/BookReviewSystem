His Dark Materials: The Golden Compass

Author: Philip Pullman

Publication Date: July 1995

Publisher: Scholastic Point

ISBN-13: 978-0590993591

Description

His Dark Materials: The Golden Compass is the first novel in the epic fantasy trilogy by Philip Pullman. The story follows Lyra Belacqua, a young girl living in a parallel universe, as she embarks on a dangerous journey to uncover the secrets behind a mysterious substance called Dust. With the help of her daemon companion, Pantalaimon, and a group of allies, Lyra discovers a world of magic, adventure, and dark forces that threaten the very fabric of reality.
