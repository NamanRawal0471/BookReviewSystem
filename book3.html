The Lord of the Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring

Author: J.R.R. Tolkien

Publication Date: July 29, 1954

Publisher: George Allen & Unwin

ISBN-13: 978-0618346257

Description

The Lord of the Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring is the first novel in the epic fantasy series by J.R.R. Tolkien. It follows the story of Frodo Baggins, a hobbit, who embarks on a perilous journey to destroy the One Ring and save Middle-earth from the dark lord Sauron. Along with a fellowship of diverse characters, Frodo faces numerous challenges and encounters fantastical creatures in his quest.
