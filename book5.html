Nineteen Eighty-Four

Author: George Orwell

Publication Date: June 8, 1949

Publisher: Secker & Warburg

ISBN-13: 978-0451524935

Description

Nineteen Eighty-Four is a dystopian novel written by George Orwell. The story is set in a totalitarian society ruled by the Party led by Big Brother. It follows the life of Winston Smith, a low-ranking member of the Party, as he rebels against the oppressive regime and attempts to maintain his individuality and freedom. The novel explores themes of government surveillance, psychological manipulation, and the power of language, leaving readers questioning the nature of truth and the consequences of totalitarianism.
